On-Demand Service

The Feature of Dmail-print

Plan Your Campaign

Tell us your goal and pick a product (postcard, letter, brochure, door hanger, envelope). Share your target area and quantity—we’ll guide you on EDDM vs Addressed Mail.

Design & Targeting

Upload print-ready art or request a custom design. We fix bleeds/safe zones, add CTA/QR, and set up ZIP/route/radius targeting so your piece reaches the right homes.

Price, List & Pay

See instant pricing by quantity. Upload your mailing list (CSV/XLSX)—or ask us to build one and validate it (dedupe, NCOA/CASS). Complete secure checkout.

Print, Mail & Track

We print, address, sort, and hand off to USPS. Most campaigns hit mailboxes in 5–7 days after approval. You’ll get status updates and mailing confirmation.
